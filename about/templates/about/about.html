About us

Who We Are

At Banyan Ayurveda we treat with traditional Ayurvedic Medicine and provide ancient Indian therapies to help heal and maintain the quality and longevity of life.

As a science of self-healing, Ayurveda encompasses diet and nutrition, lifestyle, meditation, postures, breathing exercises and medical herbs along with cleansing and rejuvenation programs for healing body, mind, and spirit.

Our Panchakarma Treatments

Our Panchakarma treatments provides the traditional cleansing therapies of Ayurveda accompanied by diet and lifestyle recommendations and support for clients.

The preparation period and methods in advance of panchakarma can provide a fulcrum point for personal transformation. By following post-panchakarma recommendations and adopting of lifestyle changes and practices, the client can embark upon a healthier and more balanced life.
